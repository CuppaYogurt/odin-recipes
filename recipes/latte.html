Latte

Usually made with 3 to 1 milk-to-espresso ratio. A good rule to follow if the beans don't provide a recipe for espresso is 18 grams in (Coffee beans) and 35 grams out (Espresso). This video by James Hoffman covers how to pull espresso shots.

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 8 oz. milk

Steps

  1. Pour espresso into desired heated latte mug
  2. Froth milk into desired temperature, creating a good amount of foam while steaming
  3. Pour milk into mug with espresso, keeping your pouring length high until there is little left that is enough to make latte art
  4. Lower pouring length and create desired art
  5. Enjoy while hot
