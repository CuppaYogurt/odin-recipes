Omelette

An omelette are cracked eggs that have been scrambled and fried, usually made with toppings.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 milk
  • Your choice of toppings

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs in a bowl, adding pepper in
  2. Add butter into a pan and melt with medium heat
  3. Once the pan is heated and butter melted, pour the eggs into the pan
  4. Once the egg has cooked a little, gently unstick the sides of the egg until you can move the egg by shifting the pan
  5. Add your desired toppings
  6. Gently fold the egg in half, covering the toppings
  7. Flip the egg slowly to cook the other side to desired texture
