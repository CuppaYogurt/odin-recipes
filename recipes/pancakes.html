Pancakes

Good ol' pancakes. So fluffy and delicious. This recipe will guide you how to make pancakes with boxed pancake mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pancake mix that needs egg and milk
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • Syrup to serve
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pour pancake mix into a bowl and mix in egg with milk then whisk together until small bubbles appear
  2. Heat a pan with butter at medium heat
  3. Once the pan has been heated, pour pancake butter into pan
  4. After a few minutes, flip the pancake
  5. Once cooked, set pancake aside, continue until all of out of batter
  6. Butter and pour syrup as desired, enjoy while hot
